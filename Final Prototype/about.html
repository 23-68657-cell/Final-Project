About the Resource Generation Office

The Resource Generation Office (RGO) is dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship within the university. We manage and operate income-generating projects that not only provide valuable resources for the academic community but also offer practical, hands-on experience for our students.

From official university merchandise to essential school supplies, our goal is to deliver quality and convenience while supporting the university's strategic objectives.
